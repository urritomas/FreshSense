How FreshSense Works

  1. Place the Sensor

    Install the compact device in each grow room, no special tools required.

  2. Continuous Monitoring

    The sensor samples air continuously and detects subtle odor changes linked to mold or bacteria.

  3. Instant Alerts

    If a risk is detected, the system sends a clear alert to your phone with recommended actions.

  4. Take Action

    Adjust ventilation, inspect trays, or remove affected areas early to protect the rest of the harvest.
